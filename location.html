Location & Directions

The wedding will be held at the Clara Barton Camp in North Oxford, Massachusetts. We hope you will stay and enjoy the facilities with us all weekend long!

Airports

The closest airport is Boston Logan (BOS), which is about a one hour drive. New York City area airports are also doable, at a three to four hour drive.

If you are staying at the Camp with us for the weekend (please do!) then you won't need a car, so let us know if you need help findingtransportation from the airport to the camp.
